Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Helix AI, operated by Mahesh Vilasagaram, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Google Account Data: Name and email when you sign in with Google (we do not store your Google password).
  • API Keys: Third-party API keys you voluntarily enter in Settings for AI features (stored securely and encrypted).
  • Academic Content: Syllabi, notes, documents, and other study materials you upload or create.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Study session durations, feature usage counts, and subscription tier.
  • Session Data: Cookies used to maintain your login session (essential cookies only).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Platform's services.
  • To personalize your study experience and AI-generated content.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To communicate important updates about the service.
  • To improve the Platform based on usage patterns.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with:

  • Instamojo: Payment processing (name and email for transactions).
  • AI Service Providers: Your study queries are sent to AI models (NVIDIA NIM, OpenAI, or Groq depending on your configuration) for processing. No personally identifiable information is included in AI requests.
  • Supabase: Database hosting provider — your data is stored securely in their infrastructure.
  • Google: If you use Google Sign-In, authentication is handled by Google's OAuth service.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase (AWS ap-south-1 region, India).
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
  • API keys are stored securely in your profile and are only used server-side.
  • All data transmission occurs over HTTPS (TLS encryption).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data through the Settings page.
  • Update: Modify your name, API keys, and AI preferences at any time.
  • Delete: Reset your account data using the "Reset Account Data" feature in Settings, which permanently deletes all your study plans, notes, and generated content.
  • Account Deletion: Request complete account deletion by emailing us.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only to maintain your login session. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts.

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact
